WHY YOU MUST VOTE

Compact yet jammed packed with voting information. For millennials and non-voters, look no further for the knowledge you need to vote with confidence your very first time.

  • You’ll learn, in a nutshell, how the U.S. Government works.
  • Politics simply explained in easy to understand language.
  • Discover the meaning of election terms, and much more.
  • Easily slips into your pocket or purse for a quick read

WHY YOU MUST VOTE gives clear cut and concise instructions on what to do, where to go, and how to cast your first ballot. Experienced voters may also find the information beneficial to them as well.

GEORGIA LYONS

Growing up in Northeastern Ohio, having traveled extensively worldwide and now living in Sothern California, Georgia Lyons is a political watcher who believes strongly in the power of citizen engagement. During her formative years, her parents worked and volunteered at polling precincts every election. She was taught early the value of her vote. In today’s world it is vitally important to be conscious, aware and involved. Georgia believes America works best when ‘We the People’ participate. In writing WHY YOU MUST VOTE, Georgia attempted to provide millennials, young people and non-voters an easy, quick, and simple voter’s guide to help them understand and navigate through the voting process with ease from the beginning to the end.
